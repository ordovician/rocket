Launch Rocket

Configure a rocket to be launched and see how far ut up goes. Here are some of the important concepts and how they impact elevation:

  • Isp - Specific impulse is a measure of fuel efficiency. Higher means better fuel efficiency.
  • Thrust - The power of the engine. Similar to horsepower of a gasoline engine. If thrust is too low, you will not be able to counter gravity.
